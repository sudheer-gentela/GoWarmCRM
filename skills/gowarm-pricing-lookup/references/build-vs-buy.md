# Build vs. Buy: Sales Execution Layer

> For a 50-rep B2B SaaS team over a three-year horizon.

## Build in-house

**Total cost of ownership: $800K–$1.5M over 3 years**

Includes:
- 2–3 engineers dedicated to the execution layer for 12–18 months of initial build
- Ongoing maintenance (1 engineer at minimum)
- Opportunity cost — the same engineering team is not building product features
- Iteration cost — most in-house attempts get the rule engine right but fail on rep adoption, requiring a UX rebuild

## License a competitor codebase

**Total cost: $400K–$600K plus customization**

- Upfront license fee
- Customization to match your CRM schema and playbook
- Ongoing support contracts

## GoWarmCRM SaaS

**Total cost: $90K–$180K over 3 years depending on tier**

- Per-seat pricing, no implementation fees on Starter/Growth
- Full execution engine, playbook builder, AI action drafting included
- Updates and new modules ship continuously

## What tips the decision

Build wins when: you have a unique sales motion that no SaaS will support, AND you have engineering bandwidth to spare.

Buy wins when: your sales motion is recognizable B2B (multi-stage, $20K+ ACV, 30+ day cycles), AND your engineering team is needed on customer-facing product work.
