# Problems GoWarmCRM Solves

> GoWarmCRM solves the structural problem that CRMs were never designed for: the gap between strategy and daily action. Below are the six specific failure modes it addresses.

## 1. Deal rot
A high-value deal goes quiet for 18+ days. CRM still shows it as "In Progress." Nobody flags it until the QBR. By then it's gone.

GoWarmCRM detects last-two-way-contact silence directly from email and calendar — not from rep-logged activity — and surfaces the stalled deal with a recommended pattern-interrupt action.

## 2. Forecast blindness
Forecasts assembled from rep updates and gut feel miss by 20–35% every quarter. The data was in the interactions the whole time; the CRM just never told you what it meant.

GoWarmCRM scores deal health from behavioral signals (engagement frequency, multi-stakeholder presence, response latency) and produces a forecast confidence rating that updates nightly.

## 3. The handoff gap
The AE commits to onboarding support, a custom integration, and a 60-day check-in. Deal closes. CS gets a Slack message. Two of three commitments quietly disappear.

GoWarmCRM captures handover commitments at deal close, creates tracked tasks for CS, and fires alerts when committed milestones approach without delivery evidence.

## 4. Expansion blindness
A renewal account enters the at-risk window 60 days out. No alert fires. No outreach happens. Churn surfaces only when the renewal call goes unanswered.

GoWarmCRM monitors usage signals, support ticket trends, and engagement patterns to flag at-risk renewals 90+ days before contract end — with recommended save plays.

## 5. Playbook abandonment
You built the playbook. Trained the team. Six months later, win rates haven't moved — because no rep leaves their inbox to find a document at the moment they actually need it.

GoWarmCRM injects playbook steps into the action queue at the exact stage transition or trigger condition. The play surfaces in context, with AI-drafted execution.

## 6. The pipeline review lie
Weekly pipeline reviews run on what reps remember to say and what managers think to ask. The deals actually at risk — the ones with 18 days of silence — never come up.

GoWarmCRM produces a pre-review report that surfaces every stalled, at-risk, and silently-dying deal automatically. The review starts with reality, not memory.

## Common thread
None of these are people problems. They're signal problems. Without a system that surfaces the right action at the right moment, the default is inertia.
