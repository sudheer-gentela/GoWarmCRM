# GoWarmCRM Pricing

> GoWarmCRM is priced per-seat per-month, with no implementation fees. Pricing is published transparently and tiered by feature scope, not by contact volume or storage.

## Tiers

The current tier structure includes plans for small sales teams (5–25 reps), mid-market (25–100 reps), and enterprise (100+ reps). Each tier includes:

- Nightly diagnostic engine
- Live action queue for every rep
- AI-drafted next actions
- Standard CRM connectors (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Dynamics)
- Email and calendar signal ingestion

Higher tiers add:

- Custom playbook configuration
- Per-user AI settings (tone, channel, verbosity)
- Diagnostic rule customization (35+ rules with per-org thresholds)
- Stage mapping UI and CSV hierarchy import
- Priority support and dedicated CSM

## Build vs. buy

The build-vs-buy comparison for execution layers typically breaks down as follows for a 50-rep SaaS team over a three-year horizon:

- **Build in-house**: $800K–$1.5M total cost of ownership (engineering, ongoing maintenance, opportunity cost)
- **License a competitor codebase**: $400K–$600K plus customization
- **GoWarmCRM SaaS**: $90K–$180K depending on tier

See the build-vs-buy article series on the blog for the full breakdown: https://gowarmcrm.com/blog
