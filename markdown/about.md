# About GoWarmCRM

> GoWarmCRM is a sales execution platform built for B2B teams that need to close the gap between defined process and rep behavior. We sit on top of your existing CRM and tell your team what to do next — every deal, every rep, every morning.

## What we believe

CRMs were built to answer "what happened?" After 30 years, they do this well. The question they were never designed to answer — "what should happen next?" — is where 20–35% of pipeline revenue disappears.

That gap can't be closed by training, hiring, or culture initiatives. It requires a structural fix: a system that runs nightly diagnostics, surfaces the right action at the right moment, and drafts the next step in the rep's workflow.

That's the category GoWarmCRM is built for. Every B2B sales team will eventually need an execution layer. We're building it.

## How we're different

We don't replace your CRM. We don't ask reps to log more data. We read signals from email, calendar, and meeting patterns directly — so the diagnostic works even when the CRM is incomplete.
