Common questions

Does GoWarmCRM replace my CRM?

No — it sits alongside your existing CRM. No rip-and-replace required. GoWarmCRM adds the execution layer that Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and Zoho don't provide natively.

How long does onboarding take?

Most Starter and Growth teams are live within 1–2 weeks. We walk you through connecting your CRM data, configuring your diagnostic thresholds, and loading your first playbooks.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time — your configuration, playbooks, and action history carry over. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.

What CRMs does GoWarmCRM work with?

Native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and Zoho. Enterprise plans include custom integration support for other CRM platforms.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a free 20-minute demo with a pipeline walkthrough rather than a self-serve trial — because the most useful thing we can show you is what GoWarmCRM would actually surface in your specific pipeline today.

How is AI billing handled?

Growth plans include GoWarmCRM's managed AI layer. Enterprise customers can bring their own API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, others) and pay AI costs directly to their provider.
