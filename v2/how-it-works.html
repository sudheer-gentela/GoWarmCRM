How GoWarmCRM Works

Three phases. One outcome: your team knows what to do next.

GoWarmCRM sits alongside your existing CRM, runs nightly diagnostics across your entire pipeline, and delivers a prioritised action queue to every rep — every morning.

From raw CRM data to daily action — automatically

No manual pipeline reviews. No weekly spreadsheets. GoWarmCRM's execution engine runs continuously, so your team always has a clear, current view of what needs to happen — and why.

Phase 01
Diagnose your pipeline every night

Rules engines run nightly across every deal, contract, case, handover, and prospect in your pipeline. Each entity is scored against configurable thresholds — stage duration, rep activity, last contact date, ARR timing — and flagged if it breaches a risk boundary.

Stall detection

Deals are scored by days in stage, last activity, and rep engagement. Thresholds are configurable per stage and deal size.

Contract risk

CLM stage changes trigger automatic action queues. Your team gets proactive alerts on contracts approaching renewal or risk windows.

Prospect hurdle scores

Every prospect gets a live hurdle score. GoWarmCRM surfaces exactly what's blocking progression and what action to take next.

Handover monitoring

Sales-to-CS commitments are tracked as first-class objects. Every promise logged, every delivery monitored automatically.

Phase 02
Surface the exact actions to take

Diagnostic alerts become a live, prioritised action queue for every rep. Unlike static task lists that go stale the moment they're generated, GoWarmCRM actions persist intelligently — snooze settings are preserved, playbook progress is tracked, and resolved items close automatically when the underlying condition clears.

Upsert-and-resolve logic

Actions are updated nightly using conflict-safe logic. Existing actions are refreshed — never duplicated — when conditions change.

Playbook triggers

When a deal moves stage, your configured playbook fires automatically. Reps see the required next step in their queue — not buried in a doc.

Priority ranking

Actions are ranked by urgency, deal value, and days-at-risk. Your team knows exactly where to start every morning.

Snooze & supersede

Reps can snooze actions with context. If conditions worsen, GoWarmCRM supersedes the snoozed action automatically.

Phase 03
AI writes the play — you approve it

Per-user AI configuration lets each rep and manager tune how intelligence is applied in their workflow. Choose your AI provider, adjust prompt behaviour per module, and keep a human in the loop for every suggested action. GoWarmCRM augments your team's judgment — it doesn't replace it.

Per-user AI settings

Every user can configure their own AI preferences — provider, tone, verbosity — with org-level defaults that cascade where no personal setting exists.

Module-level prompt tuning

AI behaviour can be tuned differently for deals vs. contracts vs. prospecting. The right tone and depth for each context.

The Rep Experience

A clear action queue. Every morning. No guesswork.

Reps don't need to triage their pipeline or decode CRM reports. GoWarmCRM surfaces the three to five actions that matter most — ranked by urgency, deal value, and time-at-risk.

Playbook steps appear exactly when they're triggered. Stall alerts fire before deals are lost. Handover tasks appear the moment a commitment is made upstream.

AI Configuration

Intelligence tuned to your team. Not a black box.

GoWarmCRM's AI layer is fully configurable at the org and individual level. Every rep can set their preferred AI provider, adjust how suggestions are written, and control when AI input appears in their workflow.

Org defaults cascade to users who haven't set preferences. Power users can override everything. The result: an AI layer that feels native to every person on your team.
