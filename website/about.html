Our Story

Built by someone who was tired of CRMs that just record things.

GoWarmCRM exists because the gap between "what happened in your pipeline" and "what you should do right now" was costing teams real revenue — and nobody was fixing it.

Most CRMs are brilliant at storing information. They'll tell you a deal moved to stage 3 on the 14th. They'll show you the contact's job title changed. They'll log every email you've ever sent.

What they won't do — what almost none of them do well — is tell you what to do next.

GoWarmCRM was built to close that gap. Not with more dashboards. Not with better reports. With a real-time action engine that watches your pipeline, applies your rules, and surfaces the exact interventions your team needs to make — before opportunities are lost.

Why we built it from scratch

We looked at the available options. Bolt-on AI layers for Salesforce. Engagement platforms like Outreach and Salesloft. Forecasting tools like Clari. Each solves a slice of the problem. None of them solves the core one: your CRM doesn't know what an action is, and it doesn't have the architecture to generate, track, and resolve them at scale.

So we built GoWarmCRM from the ground up — with the action as the primary unit of the system. Every diagnostic, every playbook step, every handover commitment, every contract risk signal flows into a single action queue. Your team always knows what needs to happen next.

Who we're building for

GoWarmCRM is built for B2B sales organisations with 10–200 reps who have outgrown a basic CRM but don't want to assemble a 12-tool stack to get intelligence out of their pipeline.

If your VP Sales is spending 3 hours a week in pipeline reviews that could be replaced by a live diagnostic — GoWarmCRM is built for you.

The core belief

A CRM that tells you what happened is a record. A CRM that tells you what to do is a system. We build systems.

Built on Node.js & PostgreSQL

GoWarmCRM is a production-grade platform — not a prototype. Deployed on Railway, served via Cloudflare, with a React frontend and a rules engine that runs nightly across your entire pipeline.

AI that stays in its lane

Every AI feature in GoWarmCRM is per-user configurable. You choose the provider, the prompt, and whether the AI recommends or acts. No black boxes. No forced automation.

Our Principles
Specificity over dashboards

A dashboard that shows "pipeline health: amber" helps nobody. An action that says "Deal X has been in stage 3 for 18 days with no activity — call the champion today" helps everyone.

Persistence over noise

We don't regenerate alerts from scratch every night. We track alert age, preserve snooze state, and resolve alerts when the underlying condition clears. Context that survives the morning.

Humans in the loop

AI features are opt-in per user, configurable per module. The system makes recommendations. Your team makes decisions. We're not building automation — we're building intelligence.
