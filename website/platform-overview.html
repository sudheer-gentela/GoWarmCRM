The Core — Actions

Your team's daily hub. Five ways actions are generated.

The Actions tab is where reps start every day. It shows a prioritised queue across all their deals — sorted by urgency, with a specific next step and channel for each one. Actions are generated in five ways:

Type A
Diagnostic Alerts
Nightly rules engine detects stale deals, missing contacts, close date risks, and unhealthy pipeline. AI enhances the description with specific context.
Type B
Playbook Tasks
When a deal moves stage or a condition is met, the next play in your configured playbook fires automatically as an action item.
Type C
Transcript Actions
Upload a Zoom, Teams, or Gong transcript and the AI extracts commitments, follow-ups, and next steps as ready-to-act items.
Type D
Email Actions
When email sync detects commitment language in a thread, AI extracts the action and surfaces it in the queue before it gets buried.

Getting Started

Most teams are live within two hours of first login.

GoWarm CRM is designed for fast time-to-value. A phased onboarding approach means you can start generating AI-powered actions on day one and reach full adoption — with playbooks, integrations, and all modules configured — within two weeks.

1
Account Setup & First Login
Accept your invitation, set your password, complete your profile, and connect your Microsoft 365 account for email and calendar sync.
Day 1 · 1–2 hours
2
Core Data Import
Import Accounts, then Contacts, then Deals via CSV. Download the template for each object, populate it, and upload. Historical activities optional but recommended.
Days 1–2 · 2–4 hours
3
Integrations
Connect Microsoft 365 for two-way email sync, calendar matching, OneDrive file access, and meeting transcript import. AI configuration requires no end-user setup.
Days 2–3 · 1–2 hours
4
Pipeline & Deal Health Configuration
Org Admin configures pipeline stages, deal health scoring weights, ARR timing rules, and team dimensions. This is what the nightly diagnostic engine runs against.
Days 3–5 · 2–3 hours
5
Team Setup & Playbooks
Invite your team, assign roles (Member or Org Admin), and build your first playbook — defining plays, channels, assignee roles, and fire conditions per stage.
Week 1–2 · variable
Dedicated onboarding manager
Every new GoWarm CRM customer is assigned a dedicated onboarding manager for the first 30 days. They'll schedule your kickoff call, guide your configuration, and make sure your team hits its first meaningful actions milestone. After day 30, your account transitions to our Customer Success team for ongoing support.
